Bio

Hi there.

I'm Kierston. I currently live in Castle Rock, CO. I'm a dental assistant turned web developer. After dabbling in the development world I decided to attend a 6 month boot camp at Galvanize. The world of web development is exciting and full of changing technologies and new advancements. I enjoy solving problems and pushing myself to overcome new challenges. I write Javascript and use the MEAN stack. I am very interested in UI/UX.

I am passionate about life and making the lives of those around me better. I'm interested in learning how I can use technology to increase the quality of life for people. I want to be a part of something bigger, something that can benefit others. I enjoy working with others and making useful contributions.

I have been married for 6 years. My husband and I met at an Ultimate Frisbee tournament while playing for our respective colleges. Our dog Nelli joined our family 3 years ago. We love everything about the outdoors, including, hiking, rock climbing, and camping. We love having adventures and experiencing new things.
